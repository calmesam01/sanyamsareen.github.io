Sanyam, a Master of Applied Computing (MAC) student at the University of Windsor, is the first and only GitHub Campus Expert in the Windsor-Essex region. When it comes to technical know-how, he is a full-stack web developer, cloud engineer, and data scientist.

As the co-founder and technical team lead at Winhacks (Windsor's first major hackathon), he led and solidified partnerships with key organizations such as Digital Ocean, independently, requiring very little supervision and guidance. Not only WinHacks, but he has also coordinated 2 additional hackathons with 500 attendees at a minimal cost that served as a bridge between job seekers and recruiters.

The interpersonal skills that he gained while organizing campus events in association with GitHub are remarkable. The Managing Employees course that he pursued in his masters played a crucial role in honing his time management and teamwork abilities where teams of random students were assigned case studies on Fortune 500 companies and every team was to present their researched work strictly in 10 minutes.
